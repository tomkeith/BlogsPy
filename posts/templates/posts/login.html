What is BookIT?

BookIT is an app that allows you to upload and store all of your books on the cloud.

How do I add books?

First, register by creating a user account. Once you register as a user, you'll be able to log in to your dashboard and add your books.

What are some Book cover best practices?

  • Have a resolution of 512x512
  • Use common image formats such as .JPG, .GIF, or .PNG
  • Remain under the 2MB limit.
  • Square images look best
